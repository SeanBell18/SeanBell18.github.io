FISHING ADVENTURE

—————————————————

Pair our quick & easy 2-night cruise to Grand Bahama Island with an exciting fishing


expedition on the glittering waters just off the coast. After indulging in all the activities our


ship has to offer during your fist day and night on board, rise early on day 2 to take


advantage of included transportation to the marina where your fishing boat awaits

Meet your friendly captain and then set for the waters offshore, which are rich in a variety


of hame species such as sailfish, swordfish, marlin, mahi mahi, wahoo and tuna, to name a


few. You'll spend approxiately 3.5 hours at sea angling for an amazing catch or simply


relaxing on deck and absorbing the magical atmosphere of the sea. If you're not quite ready


to go back to the ship after your fishing journey, enjoy extra time to shopm relax on the beach


or take part in any of Grand Bahama Island's countless activities. Just check the Shore


Excursions Desk aboard the ship for a variety of suggestions that will perfectly enhance


your day in paradise.
